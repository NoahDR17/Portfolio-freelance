About me

I'm a full stack web developer based in Liverpool with an HND in Software Development. I specialise in building websites and web applications for small businesses — from simple brochure sites to platforms with bookings, payments, and automation built in.

I work with React and Django for most projects, which means you get a fast, modern frontend and a reliable, secure backend — all built and maintained by one person who knows the full picture.
